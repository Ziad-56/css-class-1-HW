“Nothing to do if bus operators call sudden strike, cause public sufferings”

Quader on BNP's Khulna divisional rally

Road Transport and Bridges Minister Obaidul Quader today (October 23, 2022) said that he has nothing to do if private bus operators call a sudden strike and cause public sufferings. Quader, also the general secretary of ruling Awami League, gave the reply after a reporter asked him a question, referring to a sudden strike called by bus operators in Khulna ahead of BNP's Saturday rally. For all latest news, follow The Daily Star's Google News channel. He was speaking at a press conference after joining a program at Bangabandhu International Conference Centre today (October 23, 2022). Khulna District Bus-Minibus Owners' Association announced the suspension of services two days ahead of the BNP rally, but said in an official statement they were doing this in protest against the operation of three-wheelers on the highways. They withdrew the strike immediately the BNP rally ended their rally. Similarly, a transportation strike, which was not declared earlier, was enforced in the Mymensingh division ahead of the rally on October 15.
